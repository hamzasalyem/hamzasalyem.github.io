Finding Your Favorite Fragrance 

Wearing the same perfume all the time is like sporting the same haircut or eating the same food every day, you need to change it up. It is very difficult to find a different fragrance. You have to know your taste and flavor you like the most i.e. Orientals, Woody, Floral or Fresh Fragrances. Everyone has a different and unique taste.

If you love floral perfumes chances are you are a woman, as this is the most popular category in feminine fragrances. Choose an elegant Non-Alcoholic Perfume of flowers like Amiable by Mahal al Musk. Try T. Rose of fresh and Real Rose Flowers by Mahal al Musk. For Men use Abia Perfume of floral sent of tube Rose & Lavender with the heart enriched by Patchouli & Orchid, moving down to warm and woody base of White Musk and Amber. 

Oriental fragrances are exotic and mysterious as well as warm and opulent. Oriental perfumes can be quite intense, featuring warm and spicy notes like nutmeg, musk and cloves.

Fresh perfumes

Fresh perfumes can be citrus, green or aquatic and are laid back and sporty. They are perfect if you want to smell great without trying too hard and are perfect for the office. For him try Furat by Mahal al Musk if you want Fresh and sharp, simple and very masculine or Lush Sport which is a mixture of mandarin & oranges blended with Tunisian neroli, aldehydes, and for her Lush Black by Mahal al Musk is cacao flower, massoia wood, patchouli & vanilla..
